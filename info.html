Cozy, simple to use.

Safe and Protected.

Our code changes as you play, but as minimal but as secure as possible, it won't affect gameplay neither would you notice is it. We have a built in VPN system which you can allow us to enable into your client when installing our launcher, this is a secure way of keeping your IP safe.
